HOW I WORK (03)THE PROCESS
01

Diagnose before I design.

Most brand problems are positioning problems in disguise. Before I open Figma, I audit your competitive landscape, map how your audience makes decisions, and identify the visual gap your brand can own permanently. The brief you give me is a starting point, not the assignment.

02

Build the whole system.

One point of contact for identity, pitch deck, web design, and social infrastructure — no handoffs, no briefing three people who've never spoken. The system is internally consistent because one mind built all of it.

03

Hand over and step back.

Everything I deliver is documented, named, and structured so your team or the next agency can use it without calling me. I don't build dependency. The work should make you less reliant on any single designer — including me.
